Data Meetups

Data Viz DC

I am a co-organizer of Data Visualization DC, and we organize events on data communication, exploratory data analysis, new and novel ways of charting and data presentation, and tech applications in data viz. Our group is part of Data Community DC, which brings data scientists, professionals, and industry leaders together to talk anything data. Data Community DC caters to the full stack: data visualization, data science, women in data, databases and analytics, data ed, data wrangling, etc.

Learn more about Data Viz DC, Data Community DC, or other Data Community DC meetups. You can also view my meetup profile.

August 7: Data for Social Good

This is an event that I organized in collaboration with the DC/MD/VA Tableau Group. It will cover data and information in the non-profit space, and will culminate in a free-for-all mini-hack! Prizes will be provided. Starts at 5:30 PM, at the IMF's City View Lounge. Pizza and drinks will be served. RSVP on the Meetup page

July 25: Art + Data

A special Data Viz DC offering of Jennifer Beman's "Graphic Sex Project," an interactive installation that helps people think about sex in a new way. Content may be NSFW (Not safe for Work). Starts at 6:30 PM, at the White Cloud Gallery on 14th street NW. Overflow room at the Bar Pilar next door. RSVP on the Meetup page

Data Community DC Mentor-Mentee

An ongoing program of Data Community DC. Interested in becoming a mentor or a mentee? Want to be matched on your Full Stack interests? Please complete the form and we'll match you. Our matching code is open source and available on github.

June 27: Vizapalooza

Three data visualizers will showcase their work. We have a web historian, biotechnologist, and policymaker on board for this packed event! RSVP on the Meetup page

Coming soon: IRS 990 Non-Profit Data

This October, we'll be investigating potential fraud in non-profit organizations, perhaps ranking charities from best to worst, all using data viz tools. More details to come.
